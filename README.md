# Totalitas

Totalitas is a single-player, multi-themed maze-building game developed in Unity. Players construct labyrinths filled with traps and obstacles, then watch as waves of enemies attempt to navigate through. The goal is to defend your village by designing effective mazes and strategically placing traps.

Choose from a variety of characters, Monsters, Bosses, or Dragons to oversee your maze. Each character provides unique buffs and customisation options, allowing players to tailor both their character and maze to suit specific levels.

---

## Current Development

The project is in an early development stage.

- **Main Menu**: A functional UI has been implemented using Unity’s UI Toolkit.  
- **Maze Assets**: Wall presets have been created and integrated into the game. Presets are currently being developed.  
- **Characters and Enemies**: Early models are complete, with basic pathfinding and health systems under development.  
- **Traps**: Four traps have been designed so far, Nightquill, Grimfall, Lionclasp, and Endsong, each with unique mechanics.  

---

## Gameplay Loop

1. Build the maze around your village.  
2. Populate the maze with traps.  
3. Waves of enemies attempt to pass through.  
4. If enemies are defeated, the player earns rewards such as new traps.  
5. If enemies reach the village, damage is dealt, and progress is set back.  

---

## Development Notes

- The game consists of a 2.5D isometric view using 3d models with a fixed viewpoint.  
- UI design began with preliminary concepts and has evolved into a universal infinity aesthetic, symbolising the passage of time through uniquely styled levels. 
- Early challenges included high-poly models, maze logic, and pathfinding. These were addressed by reducing poly counts, agreeing on a consistent art style, and breaking coding tasks into smaller, manageable parts.
